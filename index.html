My Therapeutic Approach

I believe that therapy is a collaborative journey. My practice is rooted in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and Mindfulness. Together, we work not just to reduce "symptoms," but to help you move toward a life that feels vital, rich, and meaningful.

Whether you are facing a specific crisis or a general sense of feeling "stuck," I provide a grounded, compassionate presence to help you navigate your inner world.

Are online sessions as effective as in-person?
Research suggests that for most conditions, tele-therapy is just as effective as in-person sessions. I use a secure, medical-grade video platform to ensure your total privacy.
Is therapy covered by my insurance?
Most Swiss supplemental insurance (Zusatzversicherung) plans cover a significant portion of psychological counseling. I recommend checking your policy for "Psychotherapy by non-medical psychologists."
