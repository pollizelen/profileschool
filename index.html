Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the therapeutic process.

What should I expect from our first session? +

Our initial meeting is a chance for us to get to know each other. We will discuss what brings you to therapy now, your history, and what you hope to achieve. It's a safe space to ask questions and see if we are a good fit for your therapeutic journey.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy? +

I understand that life happens. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please provide at least 24 hours' notice. This allows me to offer the time slot to others. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice will be charged at the full session rate.

How much do sessions cost and is it covered by insurance? +

Individual sessions are 180 CHF (50 minutes). As a licensed FSP psychologist, my services are covered by most Swiss supplementary insurance (Zusatzversicherung). I recommend checking with your provider specifically regarding "Psychologische Psychotherapie" coverage.

How many sessions will I need? +

The duration of therapy varies greatly depending on your individual goals and the complexity of the issues we're addressing. Some clients find clarity in 6-10 sessions, while others benefit from long-term support. We will regularly review our progress together.

Are my sessions confidential? +

Absolutely. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of psychological practice and is protected by Swiss law. Information is only shared with your explicit written consent, except in rare circumstances where there is a serious risk of harm to yourself or others.
